Frozen desserts

Ice cream is a mixture of milk, cream, sugar,
and sometimes other ingredients, frozen into a soft, creamy delight using special techniques.
Ice cream has been a popular treat for hundreds of years
but has only become commonplace since the widespread use of refrigeration.
The exploding popularity of ice cream has led to several ice cream variations including frozen custard,
frozen yogurt, and even non-dairy versions made with
ingredients like coconut milk
